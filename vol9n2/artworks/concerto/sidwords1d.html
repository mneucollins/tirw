Centuries ago in Italy,
Masaccio, one of the first Renaissance artists to paint explicit nudes,
died at age 26 of suspected poisoning,
and Botticelli was convinced to burn his secular work
in Savonarola's bonfire.

Here, while the FBI stalked artists and writers -- including
Georgia O'Keeffe, William Faulkner,
Ernest Hemingway, Alexander Calder,
Carl Sandburg, Lillian Hellman, James Baldwin,
Marianne Moore, Allen Ginsberg, Muriel Rukeyser,
Langston Hughes and James Thurber --
the CIA's covert Cold War funding of certain artists and writers
controlled the arts
to make sure that art that was regional in subject matter was not seen;
to promote art that portrayed America in a favorable light.

I do not intend at this point to attach specific blame to any one entity,
but rather to make the case that there is a problem
that needs to be investigated.
