The things that divide us in this beautiful country
are real and important issues.
Different ways of looking at life and death.
Ways of expressing love for our fellow human beings and our country
that are very different.

Nevertheless the stark media graphic of red and blue states
is not the true story of our lives.
And in the growing understanding of what has been concealed,
I believe that the things we will agree on
will help us come together and learn to understand
each other.
