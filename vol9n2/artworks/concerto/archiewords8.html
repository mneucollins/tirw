Initiated in the secret research of World War II and the Cold War,
in laboratories across the country,
an arsenal that could change the very nature of our existence
is being developed.

When from published research, we can demonstrate their existence
and the clear potential for their abuse,
it is time to encourage informed media coverage;
to vote for and support representatives
who are tough enough to formulate policies
that will criminalize the covert use of invasive technologies
on ourselves, on animals, and on the environment.

It is time to stop the systems of interference,
to put in place an effective and continuing oversight
that looks at intelligence agencies, defense research, and industry
in terms of their impact on our country
and on the world that we share with other nations.
