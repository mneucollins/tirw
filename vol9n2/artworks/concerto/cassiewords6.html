In these times of trouble, the things that bring us together
are the shared stories of courage and friendship.
The story of Chicago Bears running backs Gayle Sayers and Brian Piccolo,
the first African American and white teammates
to room together in the National Football League.

Brian Piccolo rushing 100-yards to beat the New Orleans Saints.
Gayle Sayers' heroism in coming back from a shattering knee injury.
His record as the NFL all-time leader in kickoff returns
despite continued knee problems.
Brian Piccolo's brave battle with cancer.

The way the two men helped each other. Friendship and sports excellence
in a story where one of the heroes battled injuries throughout his career,
and the other was dead of cancer at age twenty-six.
