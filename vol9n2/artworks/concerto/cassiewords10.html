Diana knew that she was being stalked. I know what her life was like,
the feeling that you are stared at in every intimate moment.

The musicians also. Their lives like hers. Like mine.
Lived in the shadow of relentless observation.

In a time where intelligence agents of many countries
are not accountable to the citizens of the world for their actions,
remember the Irish musicians excluded from British territory in the 14th century.
Remember the Irish musicians and artists who were executed by Queen Elizabeth I.

John Lennon from Liverpool,
who spoke also for Ireland, the birthplace of his grandparents,
died in New York City.

Jimi Hendrix and Mama Cass died in London.
