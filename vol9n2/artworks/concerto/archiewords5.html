Remember

Poet Jose Marti chained in a Cuban prison by the Spanish government.
A century later, poet Heberto Padilla under house arrest in Castro's Cuba.

The means to secretly harm artists and activists,
-- not only for their point of view but also for the kind of art that they make --
could be used by many Governments
when covert weapons leave no tangible evidence,
and there is no public outcry.
