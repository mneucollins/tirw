Remember Union activist Karen Silkwood, who died in a car crash
on the way to deliver evidence of health regulation violations
at Atomic Energy Commission contractor Kerr McGee;

Polish hero, General Wladyslaw Sikorski,
killed in a plane crash after he called for an investigation of the Katyn massacre;

writer, teacher, and influential environmental activist Wallace Stegner,
who died in an automobile accident in New Mexico;

performance artist Spalding Gray,
who criticized US policy in Swimming to Cambodia,
and died in the waters of the East River.
