For who among us would support a nightmare scenario
in which our heavenly environment,
and the weather that nurtures us,
are usurped by defense scientists of opposing armies,
that would turn our shared world
into a battleground?

The skies in which at night we see the stars
and in the morning the sun rises,
the rain that comes at the will of the heavens,
the gift of snow falling through the night,
warm breezes on the beach
were not created as weapons of warfare.
