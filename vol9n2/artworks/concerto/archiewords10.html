There are things that those of us who are under attack
know are happening. We have studied the research.
We have been hurt by the torture.
We have experienced the invasion of our lives.

Covert torture
that uses unseen techno- or neuro-weapons to inflict pain.

Manipulation of our lives, our careers, our relationships,
that if not Government sponsored,
is tolerated by the Government.

These things will become more evident
when the stories of those who have been hurt
and of those who have knowledge of the systems and technologies
are not suppressed.
