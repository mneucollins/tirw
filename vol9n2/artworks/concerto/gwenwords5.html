And now the feeling that what I am going to write
is being anticipated.

I envision a neural interface that when fed data about future events
would -- using an exact model of her brain -- model a writer's response.

And then, as if a writer worked on a terrible psychological battleground
for which a cowardly enemy held the knowledge of her battle plan...

No matter what the stated purpose of the research
its real intent -- to silence writers, to silence this writer --
was apparent.
