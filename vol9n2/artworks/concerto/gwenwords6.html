My life now led on a psychological battleground
where every word I write is created
in skirmish with some despicable ancient cult.

Malicious neuro-surveillance generated playback theater.
Intelligence agency slight of hand,
developed during World War II at Camp X.

Half asleep I recalled an electromagnetic research laboratory.
The lab had a NASA contract to investigate the D-layer of the atmosphere,
a place where communications were lost in spaceship reentry.
In the center of an enormous room was a blue vacuum chamber,
where the plasma that simulated the D-layer of silence
was housed.

(As if we were purposely put in the path of some ancient vendetta,
I have always felt that we -- my first husband, who was Catholic, and I
-- were herded into break up
almost from the very beginning.)
