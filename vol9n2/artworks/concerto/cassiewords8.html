There are losses of human spirit that take something intangible from all of us.
The death of Britain's Princess Diana.

In a time where intelligence agents of many countries
are not accountable to the citizens of the world for their actions;

when British agents can spy on American citizens
and then give the information to American intelligence agents;

when US intelligence agents can spy on British citizens
and then turn the information over to British intelligence agents,

the US National Security Agency
collected over a thousand pages of classified information
about Princess Diana.
