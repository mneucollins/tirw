The spirit of John Muir walks with us
in the meadows, forests, valleys and mountains
but not in the Hetch Hetchy Valley of the Tuolumne River.

The grassroots campaign to halt the ruin of Yosemite's Hetch Hetchy Valley,
failed.
The River was damned; the Valley was flooded.
A year later, John Muir died suddenly of Pneumonia.

He lived the life that he wrote, walking the woods.
drawing, writing every detail of the forest.

He left us the gift of eternal landscape
forever free and open.
