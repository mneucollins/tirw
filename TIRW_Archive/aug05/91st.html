DE AMERICAAN DIE IK NOOIT GEWEEST BEN
THE AMERICAN I NEVER WAS
Chris Keulemans

The Dutch writer-musician-cultural entrepreneur Chris Keulemans (IWP' 01)  has for years been building a stunning air castle out of his and others' memory fragments of pop America....people, we never looked this good, warts and all!!!
 
http://www.deamerikaan.nl/
 
Read, too, Keulemans' brilliant short play "Albanians"  (2001) in which he lucidly anticipates the violent clashes between locals and immigrant others that have been wracking the Netherlands in the last few years.
