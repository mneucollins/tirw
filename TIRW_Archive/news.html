THE IOWA REVIEW WEB

Publishing work in and about new media since 1999, The Iowa Review Web is well-known
for its commitment to new writing. The site is sponsored by the English
Department and the Graduate College at the University of Iowa, and the School of
Journalism and Mass Communication at the University of Minnesota.

TIR Web adds new work to our site every three months.
