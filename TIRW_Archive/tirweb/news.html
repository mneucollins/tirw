THE IOWA REVIEW WEB

Publishing electronic literature since 1999, The Iowa Review Web is well-known for its commitment to new writing. The site is sponsored by the English Department and the Graduate College at the University of Iowa.

Starting in 2002, TIR Web expanded. It currently includes--along with electronic literature--other varieties of experimental writing and art. It also features interviews with innovative writers and artists, and critical articles and essays.

TIR Web adds new work to our site every three months.

91° MERIDIAN, A PUBLICATION FROM THE UI INTERNATIONAL WRITING PROGRAM, IS ONLINE

The University of Iowa International Writing Program (IWP) is a one-of-a-kind residency program that brings together the writers of the world. In 2004 the IWP marks 38 years as a facilitator of intellectual interaction, a promoter of global understanding, an advocate of literary freedom, and a celebrant of the importance of writers everywhere.

eXchanges, A LITERARY E-JOURNAL DEVOTED TO THE ART OF TRANSLATION, IS ONLINE

eXchanges is the University of Iowa's literary e-journal devoted to translations both into and out of the English language. The publication aims to foster cultural interchange and expand awareness of translation as an art form. Publishing the source text alongside its translation, the journal also highlights the symbiotic nature of the process.
