Clara to me,


June 22, 1990

Dear Laura,

It seems hard to believe that Mikhail and I have been "ladno" now for almost ten years, and yet we have hardly been together a span of three weeks.  And at that, we have not been given permission to marry, yet. 

But I am writing to you from the White Sea, where Mikhail is working again in Severodvinsk. I was allowed to come up here for the summer--we have a cottage on an island near Arkhangelsk.  Last night was the Solstice, "our night", and although we did not have a ceremony, we sat outside a cathedral until the dawn, planning our future. 

If all goes well, Mikhail will return to St. Petersburg in a year.  I believe that the authorities will allow me to buy the apartment, and we will have a place to live. 

Oh, I do wish you could see the nights here.  Rainbows of light, amber and white. 

Love, Clara
