On Reconsideration:



Between the lines of a few simple letters, a story of love.  The news reports a country in decline, rumors of government chaos, abandoned hidden cities, economic boom followed by unexpected collapse.

But I have been distracted, ill for much of the year.  You may judge for yourself.

September, 1999.
