Clara to me,


May 11, 1986

Dear Laura:

I have wonderful news to report! Mikhail and I have finally received permission from both governments to marry. Once the official permission was obtained--not without a great amount of trouble on Royce's part, high-level string-pulling, you know the ropes--the other problems solved themselves. I am able to keep my job here (very important), and I have even been given permission to accompany Mikhail this summer as his research assistant. There is much new activity in the northeast, and Mikhail is scheduled to travel to key areas of interest to our government. Altogether we will visit five resource areas over the summer months. A kind of honeymoon. Also, official sanction made it possible for us to secure housing. I have liked living with Alyona, but I long for my own kitchen again, just to make a cup of tea for Mikhail by ourselves. We will have an apartment in the block housing, but it will be ours.
So, raise a glass of champagne on the summer solstice. That's the big night!

Love, Clara
