Mikhail to me,

14 July, 1999

Dear Laura,

As close friend to Clara Tatiana, I write to you.  Clara came to me in Pevek this spring.  I am sorry to tell how she was very sick for many weeks.   In the end of June, she died away.  I know how sad we are all.  I am now in Siberia, but I will contact you when I return to St. Petersburg. 

Mikhail Berglund
