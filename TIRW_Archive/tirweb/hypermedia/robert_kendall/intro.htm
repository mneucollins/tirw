Introductory Note

 

This poem requires a browser that supports frames and JavaScript.

Because text is displayed simultaneously in different frames, the colors of links will sometimes not accurately indicate whether or not they lead to unread text. To update the link colors at any point in the reading, click on your browser's Reload or Refresh button.

Occasionally, clicking on a link will seem to have no effect. This is because the link leads to text that is already displayed in another frame. Clicking on the Reload or Refresh button will hide these inactive links.
