No tricks, too late for wordgames, everything in Cibachrome and backlit. Like a Czech theatre ensemble we take our places in the dark as a synth calliope plays the overture: Petruchka and Pollyanna, ours is a nostalgically cross-cultural pairing. I.e., any difference is a gulf. What's next is when courtship or seduction goes awry. Would it be any different were you able to imagine the hours of waiting, the newspapers, the dust, even the moistest of bodies eventually worn to chaff?
