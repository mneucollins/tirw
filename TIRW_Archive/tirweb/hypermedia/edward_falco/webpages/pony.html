In the middle of the block, holding on to the fire hydrant for support, we played johnny-on-the-pony: three of us (the pony) bent over and held each other by the waist as the rest, one at a time, ran and jumped on as hard as they could. The object of the game to see how many boys the pony can carry before it crumbles under one final run and leap.
