The Birth of Detachment


Raphael:

Who more beautiful than a mother, 
a woman made so without the touch of man,  
a mother who gave birth to a son who borrowed 
all too human flesh for a purpose divine? 
See the circle, her arms, her robes, how they enfold him,
give him a secure place of emergence.
I am merely a drop of water, hoping not to mar
her surface, but to reflect her beauty.
