What is a Walk-In?

Simply put, a walk-in is a soul who is invited to complete a life in progress by a soul who wants out or, is simply done with the life. While walk-ins do not assume the karma of the outgoing soul, they do take responsibility for the things that have already been set in motion ie: family responsibilities, children, etc. The exchange is always for a specific service to humanity and is never forced. There is evidence to suggest that agreements are made between lifetimes. How did this happen?

Some walk-ins say that their host soul never walked out, that the one who walked in was not a different soul but merely a higher aspect of their own oversoul who came to begin the service.

Others say they are sharing a body with another dimensional being who is wiser, more spiritually enlightened. One who can see the overall picture and guide the work. Still others say their bodies have been host several soul exchanges, each completing a task and taking the consciousness higher. Whether there is a merging or an actual exchange, the person is in for a great deal of change, turmoil and confusion, because the exchange is rarely a conscious one.

What changes occur ?

Although walk-ins go through tremendous upheaval and change, there may be other causes for such events, such as spontaneous kundalini rising, a step up in spiritual awareness, seeming mental instability, chemical imbalance and so forth. Other walk-ins and some psychics can usually tell that there is a different kind of 'depth' in the eyes. or, perhaps a seeming vacancy.

There seems to also share other common-alities... most divorce the spouse of the former occupant unless the spouse moves ahead with them spiritually. Almost all change their name - taking on or adopting - a spiritual name and many move to a new location.

Where do walk-ins come from?

So far we know of five origins (or vibrations) that walk-ins come from - as reported by walk-ins themselves - those who come from a hierarchic origin (Christian, Buddhist, etc.), those who come from an Interplanetary Alliance (Pleiades, Arcturus, Sirius, etc.) those who come from a Group Soul origin (Oversoul, Master Soul, Family Soul, etc.) and those who come from a Universal origin (Spirit, Creative Energy, Universal Mind., etc.) or, those who come from the Angelic Realm.
