A frequent dream: My family gathers for dinner at Woolworth's. The dream is black and white; therefore, the scene must take place before the advent of color TV. There is a war bonds poster for sale on the wall, but my family has never been much for politics and my mother has a rule: we shall only talk about pleasant things at the table, like her nervous breakdown. Tonight, the topic is my cooking, and my sister wants to know what I put in the osso buco. When I refuse to tell, she starts to cry.
