Millie Niss's poetry and web installations have been widely read and seen. She was included in a show of visual poetry at Dudley House, Harvard University and has been a featured reader at The Screening Room and for Just Buffalo Literary Center's Open Reading series.  Her website is www.sporkworld.org. 

Martha Deed has published electronic poetry and digital art at www.arteonline.arq.br and www.erasures.net.  Her web site is www.sporkworld.org/Deed.
