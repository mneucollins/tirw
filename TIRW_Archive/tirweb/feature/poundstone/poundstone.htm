William Poundstone is the author of eight books--two of them, The Recursive Universe and Labyrinths of Reason, were nominated for the Pulitzer Prize. His new book, The Redmond Interview,will be published by Little, Brown in 2003. Poundstone's digital photographs will be shown at Circle Elephant Art in Los Angeles this October.

Brian Kim Stefans is the author of three books of poetry, including Free Space Comix (Roof, 1998). His book of poetic essays, Fashionable Noise: On Digital Poetics, is due from Atelos Books at the end of 2002. He edits the website Arras, devoted to new media poetry.
