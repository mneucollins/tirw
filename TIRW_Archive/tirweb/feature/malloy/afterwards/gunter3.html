Gwen, I want to tell you what happened
but it is not easy to explain.
I was in town. It was Spring, and I felt free.
Remember, it had been a long Winter.
I went to the pub.
The one with tables overlooking the river
where we always went.
I was drinking a beer by myself.
A woman came over to my table
and sat down.
She said she needed a ride to San Francisco.
