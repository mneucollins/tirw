Motomichi Nakamura is a visual artist living in NYC. He graduated from Parsons School of Design and he specializes in creating animations and visuals for online/offline use. His animations have been showcased at The Sundance Online Film Festival 2001 and 2002; and the Beaubourg Contemporary Art Center. His award-winning work has been published in many art magazines in both the U.S. and in Europe.

YOUNG HAE CHANG HEAVY INDUSTRIES (http://www.yhchang.com) was founded in Seoul by Young-hae Chang, and Marc Voge.
