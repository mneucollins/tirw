Mark Amerika is a professor of digital art at the University of Colorado. His work has been exhibited internationally at many well-known venues, including the Walker Arts Center, the Guggenheim, and the Whitney Biennial. His FILMTEXT is the third part of a major new media trilogy. It was commissioned by Playstation 2 as part of his net art retrospective at the Institute for Contemporary Arts in London.
