Nicolas Clauss was born in Paris in 1968 and lives there still. He is a painter who stopped "traditional" painting to use multimedia and the internet as a canvas. His recent work is displayed on the site flyingpuppet.com. He works as a freelance multimedia artist on commissioned projects.

Jean-Jacques Birgé is a sound designer and a music composer. He is also a film director; his film, "The Sniper, Sarajevo a Street under Siege" received a British Academy Award.
