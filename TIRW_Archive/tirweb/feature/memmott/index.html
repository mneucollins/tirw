Talan Memmott is a hypermedia artist/writer from San Francisco, California. He is the Creative Director and Editor of the online hypermedia literary journal BeeHive. His website is http://memmott.org/talan/

M.D. Coverley (Marjorie Coverley Luesebrink) writes electronic hypermedia fiction and non-fiction. Her full-length novel, Califia, is available on CD-ROM from Eastgate Systems. She is currently President of the Board of Directors of the Electronic Literature Organization.
