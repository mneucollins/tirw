Kim White is a text artist who works with experimental book forms. She holds an MFA in creative writing from Columbia University and has written for both print and electronic media. Her “books” include Scratching for Something, The Minotaur Project, The New Disease, and Stories of Chloe Roman (forthcoming). She is currently working as a research fellow at The Institute for the Future of the Book www.futureofthebook.org co-located at Columbia University and the University of Southern California.

Sarah Townsend is a PhD candidate in Language, Literacy and Culture at the University of Iowa and holds an MFA in Poetry from the Iowa Writers’ Workshop.
