The University of Iowa's International Writing Program Presents The Third Annual Paul Engle Day Festival

A Conference at the University of Iowa, October 11-12, 2002

NEW MEDIA POETRY
Aesthetics, Institutions & Audiences

The conference will focus on poetry composed for digital environments, exploring cross-disciplinary and cross-cultural accounts of this work in the broader context of contemporary arts and culture.

Speakers and artists include: Giselle Beiguelman, John Cayley, Kenneth Goldsmith, N. Katherine Hayles, and Marjorie Perloff.

For more information visit the conference website.
