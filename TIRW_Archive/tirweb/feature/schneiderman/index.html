Davis Schneiderman is Chair of the American Studies Program and an Assistant Professor of English at Lake Forest College. His creative work has been nominated for a Pushcart Prize and has been accepted by Exquisite Corpse, Quarter After Eight, The Little Magazine, Neotrope, Happy, Gargoyle, EnterText, 3AM, and The Café Irreal, and Mammoth Press's forthcoming anthology Sudden Stories, among others. He is currently co-editing the collection, Millions of People Reading the Same Words: William S. Burroughs and the Global Order (Pluto Press).
