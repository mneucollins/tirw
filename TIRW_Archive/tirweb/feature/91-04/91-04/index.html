New work from the current issue of 91st Meridian

Hualing Nieh Engle was the co-director, with Paul Engle, of the IWP, from its founding in 1967 until 1989. Their dialogue, detailing some of Paul’s pre-IWP life as the director of the Iowa Writers’ Workshop (1943 to 1966), is part of her new memoir “Three Lives,” (Beijing 2004).
