ABOUT"INFLAT-0-SCAPE: AN EXPLORATION OF ARCHITECTURE, TECHNOLOGY, ECONOMY, AND OTHER THINGS THAT GO BOOM"

Inflat-o-scape is an experimental, multi-faceted 'view' of an
urban information zone: a merging of architectural structures, information technologies and inflatable forms. Inspired by the disjuncture between the seamless, virtual and abstract promise of new technologies and my own lived experience in a vast, multi-faceted, and at turns an utopian and distopian metropolis like Los Angeles.

Inflat-o-scape is organized into six related and overlapping areas:

SPEECH - the speech bubble as it relates to digital technologies, collective communications and boosterism as architecture.

CLUSTERS - landscape as mapped by consumer trends and
marketing technologies, the bubble economy and inflation as a
crowd phenomenon.

COMPRESSION - networks as cities, data transfer mechanics and the relationship speed to time.

PROGRESS - visionary and failed prototypes of inflatable architecture, architectural experimentation and inter-related social utopias and disasters.

SUBCONSCIOUS - breathing technologies, iconographies of weightlessness, and gravity as collective space.

APPENDAGE - temporal forms as architectural appendages, the progressive ideal as globalized commodity, the collapsed and disposable object.

Funded as an new media artist project by Creative Capital.
