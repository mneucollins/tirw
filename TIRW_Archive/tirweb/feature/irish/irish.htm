Jessica Irish is an inter-media artist, working in multimedia, print and online spaces. Her current work is an investigation into the relationships between information technology, architecture and what is still called 'the landscape'. Irish is the founding Co-Director for OnRamp Arts, a digital arts studio for collaborative projects between communities and artists in Los Angeles.
