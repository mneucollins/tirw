Heidi Bean is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of English at the University of Iowa.

Seth Thompson is a media artist and educator whose work has been exhibited internationally. He has worked as a Contractual Artist/Lecturer with the Metropolitan Museum of Art and as Education Director at Harvestworks Digital Media Arts, and he is currently the director/producer of Wigged Productions and a part-time faculty member at the University of Akron.

geniwate is an electronic text artist from Melbourne, Australia. Her work has appeared in many electronic journals and she is a winner of the trAce-/alt-X International Hypertext Competition and a finalist in the Electronic Literature Organization's electronic poetry award.

Deena Larsen has been writing new media literature for over a decade. She conducts writing workshops and hosts chats for the Electronic Literature Organization. She works for the U.S. government as a technical writer. Her website is www.deenalarsen.net/

Pamela Gay specializes in short fiction, creative nonfiction, and installation art. She teaches "Enlarging Micro-Fiction: Text & Image" at Binghamton University, State University of New York where creative writing and graphics arts students collaborate on an e-anthology incorporating text & image.
