Heidi Bean is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of English at the University of Iowa.

Seth Thompson is a media artist and educator whose work has been exhibited internationally. He has worked as a Contractual Artist/Lecturer with the Metropolitan Museum of Art and as Education Director at Harvestworks Digital Media Arts, and he is currently the director/producer of Wigged Productions and a part-time faculty member at the University of Akron.
