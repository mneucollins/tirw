Based in Seoul, South Korea, YOUNG-HAE CHANG HEAVY INDUSTRIES combines text with jazz to create Web-based New Media work. Writing in three different languages, Young Hae Chang and Marc Voge strip away interactivity, graphics, photos, illustrations, and colors to leave viewers with language and sound.

Read Distance, Homelessness, Anonymity, and Insignificance," an interview with YOUNG-HAE CHANG HEAVY INDUSTRIES by Thom Swiss
