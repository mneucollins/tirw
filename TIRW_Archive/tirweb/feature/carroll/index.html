Amy Sara Carroll Amy Sara Carroll is a doctoral candidate in Duke University's Program in Literature. Currently she is completing her dissertation on contemporary Mexican and U.S. postmodern cultural production, including performance, installation, video and net-art. Carroll has published poetry and poem-prints in various journals, and her poem-print "Interracial" recently appeared in This Bridge We Call Home (Routledge, 2002). She has exhibited poem-prints at the Audre Lorde Project (Brooklyn, New York), Duke University Museum of Art, Schweinfurth Memorial Art Center (Auburn, New York), and State-of-the-Art Gallery (Ithaca, New York).

Heidi Bean is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of English at the University of Iowa.
