Judd Morrissey received his MFA from Brown University. His digital work has been internationally exhibited; it has also been reviewed in The New York Times and The New Republic.

Lori Talley's work in electronic literature has been included in a number of international exhibitions including ISEA97, P0es1s: International Exhibition of Digital Poetry, and DAC2001. Talley teaches at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.

Jessica Pressman is Associate Director of the Electronic Literature Organziation and a doctoral student at UCLA's English Department, where she is writing a dissertation on electronic literature.
