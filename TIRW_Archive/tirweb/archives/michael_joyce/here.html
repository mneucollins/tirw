"Here" is a mystery, a familiar face but as yet without a name. Memory idles like a small engine, catches or doesn't. Recurring memories of the S-Bahn stop near the film school in Münich, young graphics students with knapsacks and neon colored hair. Here is where. Years hence they will come back to this space, these same low cream brick classroom buildings with their dull aluminum casements, and feel nothing of the immediacy of the cigarette inhaled and flicked now toward the street. A small engine: he simply cannot recall the term for the architectural style whose blandness characterized the end of the twentieth century. International.
